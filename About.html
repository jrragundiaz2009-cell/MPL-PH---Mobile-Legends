The Mobile Legends: Bang Bang Professional League Philippines (MPL PH) is the premier esports league for Mobile Legends: Bang Bang in the country, and the standard on the global stage. Since its inception in 2018, MPL PH teams have dominated international tournaments, after Fnatic ONIC PH secured fifth consecutive world championship titles by 2024. Witness the power of Lakas ng Pinas in every game!

Operating under a franchise-based system, MPL PH remains the direct path for Filipino MLBB squads eyeing spots in major events such as the Mobile Legends: Bang Bang Mid-Season Cup and the M-Series World Championship. Each year is split into two dynamic seasons, ensuring there’s never a dull moment. Stay updated with MPL PH—the heart of MLBB esports in the Philippines!

Over the years, fan-favorite teams like Sunsparks, Blacklist International, APBren, and ECHO Philippines have risen to prominence, representing the Philippines on the world stage. Follow your favorite teams and players on their journey to unleash their hero within as they battle for glory and uphold the esteemed Filipino MLBB legacy.

Rooted in the spirit of unity and the Filipino passion for competition, MPL PH stands as a thriving community of gamers, fans, and future champions. Be part of the MPL PH community, and join us as we keep the fires of MLBB esports burning bright in the Philippines and beyond!
