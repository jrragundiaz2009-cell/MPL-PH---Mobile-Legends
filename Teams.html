PARTICIPATING TEAMS

  • Team Liquid Philippines

    •       

    Team Liquid PH may have lost a world championship finals MVP-caliber gold laner, but they are entering this season with another of the same caliber. Despite a less successful showing last season, a Cavalry stacked with world champions can never be underestimated.

  • Onic Philippines

    •       

    ONIC PH enters this season with a powerhouse roster ready to solidify their legacy. The team’s journey aligns with key storylines, from their deep-rooted history in MPL PH to their evolving playstyle. With a mix of experience and emerging talent, the Super Family aims to once again dominate through strategic synergy and standout performances, making them a formidable force this season.

  • Twisted Minds PH

    •       

    Twisted Minds embodies the essence of a newcomer—a fresh organization with a roster of players teaming up for the first time. Despite their inexperience as a unit, the players and coaches bring seasoned expertise. The challenge lies in how they will forge their chemistry and identity as they step into the season.

  • Aurora Gaming

    •       

    In just their second season, Aurora has risen from newcomers to title contenders, standing out as the only team to push the reigning MPL PH and world champions to their limits. With an unchanged roster heading into the new season, the big question is whether they can maintain—or even surpass—their current position.

  • Team Falcons

    •       

    Gone are the days when the players who brought home the Philippines' fourth world championship title wore black and yellow—this season, they aim to soar higher. While Team Falcons may be newcomers in name, their strong third-place finish last season also cements their status as serious contenders.

  • AP BREN

    •       

    AP Bren, one of the most established esports organizations in the Philippines, enters this season as a newcomer due to its revamped roster. While Der, kkd0t, and Coach Pakbet bring prior league experience, this marks their debut under the AP Bren banner with this player mix. Fresh off a championship win in MDL Philippines Season 4, the team’s core players are eager to prove themselves against the nation’s best.

  • TNC Pro Team

    •       

    Returning from his stint in Indonesia, Yawi joins TNC to fuel their rise, bringing a world champion's edge and igniting rivalries with players across the league. Meanwhile, Zaida, who was once a literal cavalry, will now shine in the front line of the Phoenix Army.

  • Smart Omega

    •       

    Smart Omega’s roster this season is filled with familiar faces, save for their substitute player. Notably, the reigning MPL PH and world champions, ONIC PH, suffered their last match loss at the hands of the core members of this lineup. While their previous playoffs performance fell short, their proven capabilities over multiple seasons make them a team to watch.
